About Odarkor

Odarkor is a website dedicated to showcase the achievements of African heroes. It relies on the contribution from it's members to bring to light the somewhat hidden achievements of African heroes.


FAQ

Odarkor is a name. It's the name of a lady who during the development of this project was of great help. I'm certain that this project wouldn't be here at a time like this if it wasn't for her.
